Focus areas:

  • Whenever facing SMGS if possible try to maintain and increase your distance between you and the enemy if you have a Deagle
  • Get a teammate to follow you when you are going agro, I am not saying stop going agro but get someone to follow you so they can trade, having someone following you can help in multiple ways such as boosting your confidence in the actual fight because if you die you don't have to worry about having a meaningless death.
  • When running up second middle expect the apps peek and have your weapon out, honestly the 1 second it will add to your apps push it worth it when you consider if they peek with a flash and you have no gun you are dead.
  • Banana aggression is one of your favorite things to do and if you get a teammate to flash and someone to follow you this can hugely increase your chances of this play working more consistently.
  • Whenever you drop utility and there is a chance of an enemy being close try to hide or move out of LOS (line of sight) so if they do peek trying to catch you without your gun, you are safe.
  • When sitting behind a smoke make sure you are not in line of a common angle otherwise you stand a high chance of getting spammed.
  • Whenever you are low try to play passive as just having your presence in the round is intimidating because 3v2 looks alot scarier than 2v2 with one -20 hp from you attempting to kill them.
  • If you see the enemies are playing aggressive always judge whether you will be able to get the utility down before they peek as if they peek and see you holding utility that is a free kill for them and a gun in your hand will usually always do more damage than a nade/flash/smoke.
  • Generally speaking aggression when the enemy is on a light buy is not optimal as in most situations you will be closing the distance and weaker weapons are more effective at closer range, just something to be aware of.
  • One of the most important skills to master is visualization at a glance of the mini-map you should be able to understand all the open positons and all the positions your teammates are covering and all the possible positions an enemy could be at, there were a few times when you were caught off-guard from enemies being in positions that were unexpected or positions you thought your teamamtes were holding.
