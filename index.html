Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 10/6/2022

Recommendations

  • I can clearly see that larger flicks and tracking enemies is quite clearly uncomfortable and strained the reason you probably don't feel it is because you have gotten used to the sens but it is obvious when watching you, this will lead to you losing more fights that are outside of the "Comfortable" zone for flicking on your sens, with a lower sens this comfort zone gets increasingly smaller and smaller.
  • I understand that copying the pro settings is not going to immediately make you better but there are several reasons these professionals pick their sensitivity and its not just because it felt good, currently out of about 400 professional players only 6 use below 500edpi and you are currently on 337.5 and the average is 800edpi
  • The position you play on T side as an entry, is not compatible with low sens at a high level, this is because as an entry you are required to clear many angles as quickly and efficiently as possible and having such a low sens will cause you to do what's called "lazy" clearing this is when you do the motions but are not focused on every peek and when an enemy is in the angle you are clearing they will have the advantage, also again looking at the professionals you will notice that the vast majority of dedicated entry players have an average edpi of 750-950 and this is for a good reason as at this sens they can full focus clear every angle without having to do extreme swipes.

Focus areas:

  • Use Utility lineups wherever possible as this will increase the consistency if where they land and this will thus increase your consistency in plays you do involving the utility.
  • Put yourself in positions to BE traded and TO trade your teammates, if you notice someone is going agro go with them to back them up this will allow them to be more confident in any fights they take as they trust you to kill the enemy that does kill them, also make sure that you are not too close as this makes it easy for the enemy to get a double spray kill or too far as this will allow the enemy to escape before you can shoot at them and trade, so constantly monitor and adjust your positioning from your teammate to allow you to be within an optimal range for trading, alternatively try to get someone to follow you as much as possible as make sure you keep yourself within their tradable range, don’t go to far too fast otherwise when you do engage an enemy and die you teammate will be too far to trade you.
  • When dropping utility, make sure 95% of the time you are only throwing it when you are completely safe and there is no chance of an enemy peeking you while you have utility out, because Guns will always do more damage than any utility you throw, Utility should be used for damaging people in positions you can’t see, not ones you can. IF you do need to throw a dangerous piece of utility that exposes you, make sure you clear the angle first making sure there is no signs of an enemy, no jiggle peeking, etc and then as quickly as possible pull out the utility line it up and throw it as soon as the utility leaves your hand, duck behind cover and pull out your weapon, it is very common for an enemy to peek the exact position from where a piece of utility has been throw as you have effectively given up your positional advantage.
  • When on Pistols (Not Deagle) and SMGS, it is important to play angles that allow you to take full advantage of them, and using the “Throwing distance” we can easily picture whether an angle will be within the optimal range for our weapons, I want you to focus on always putting yourself in an advantageous fight whenever possible and this means using off-angles, and using positions that allow your weapons the highest chance of success.
  • Practise Rifle spray patterns and I recommend starting to use the “Spray for the head” Technique this involves changing your spray pattern slightly as most players after the first 3-5 bullets pull down to the chest, the above technique forces you to keep your spay high and aim for headshots, this is a technique that all professional players are using now as it is incredibly deadly and allows for more consist kills than bursting or tapping, I recommend practising this technique exclusively in Normal FFA Deathmatch (Not headshot only) against real players, as this will allow your practised technique to transfer to the real game a lot better.
